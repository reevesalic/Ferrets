Finding a Vet

Finding a quality vet can be very difficult. You need to find someone that is an exotic vet.
(I travel 2 hours (an hour each way) for a good vet. Even if the person that answers the phone says they see ferrets,
they may not be very knowledgable.

You can interview the veterinarian and ask questions to find out if they are knowledable.
The first quesion I ask the vet is what can they tell me about treating a ferret for adrenal disease
or what are the symptoms of insulinoma.
