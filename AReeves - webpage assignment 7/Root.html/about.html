About Us

Since 2006, we have been owned by 25 ferrets. Since ferrets only
live on average between 5-7 years, it is difficult for some to handle the heart break.
We fell in love with ferrets when our son asked for a ferret for Christmas.
We adopted 2 on Christmas Eve 2006. We lost one of our first ferret when he was only
2 years old. We lost some that were almost 8 years old.
Ferrets are amazing pets that require a lot of love, care, attention, and give back so much more.
